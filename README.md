## Project Highlights

### Business Insight Extractor
A prompt engineering system designed to extract structured, decision-ready insights from unstructured business text. Focuses on Chain-of-Thought, role prompting, and clear output formatting.

### Document Q&A with RAG (Improved)
Moved from a clean book-based RAG demo to real Apple SEC filings (10-K and 10-Q). Includes document cleaning, a fixed evaluation test set, and qualitative scoring of retrieval quality.

### Multi-Agent Business Research System
A three-agent system that researches a business topic, writes a structured report, and then critiques its own output. Built with LangGraph.
